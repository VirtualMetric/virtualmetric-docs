# Licensing

**VirtualMetric** offers the following licensing options:

|Edition|Features|
|---|---|
|_Community_|Up to **100GB** daily. For technology enthusiasts. No UI|
|_Basic_|Up to **500GB** daily. For home lab enthusiasts, researchers, and data explorers|
|_Standard_|Up to **2TB** ingest. For businesses and consultants|
|_Enterprise_|Unlimited ingest. For large enterprises and system integrators|
