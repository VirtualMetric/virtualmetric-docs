# Managing Accounts

The **Account Settings** page allows you to manage your personal information, preferences, and security settings.


## Access

- Click your profile icon on the top right corner to open the profile menu
- Click the **Account Settings** link from the dropdown menu

The Account Settings page is organized into three main sections.

## Profile Settings

This section contains your basic account information. Each field can be edited by clicking the **Edit** link on the right side of the field.

### Email Address

Your primary contact email address for account notifications and login credentials.

### Full Name

Your display name throughout the DataStream interface.

### Title

Your professional designation or role title.

## Preferences

This section allows you to customize how DataStream displays information. These settings are user-specific and don't affect other users.

### Display Language

Select your preferred interface language from the available options.

### Timezone

Set your timezone to ensure accurate timestamp display throughout the interface.

### Date and Time Format

Choose your preferred date and time display format (e.g., English United States, ISO 8601).

### Time Precision

Select the time precision level for timestamps (Seconds, Milliseconds, Microseconds, or Nanoseconds).

### Display Theme

Choose between Light, Dark, or Auto themes to customize the interface appearance.

## Password

This section allows you to update your login password.

:::caution
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a special character. You cannot reuse your last 5 passwords. Password changes are required every 180 days.
:::

To change your password:

1. Click the **Edit** link next to the Password field
2. Enter your **Current Password** for verification
3. Enter your **New Password**
4. **Confirm New Password** by retyping
5. Click **Save** to update

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem:** Changes won't save when clicking Edit
**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Verify you have edit permissions

**Problem:** New password is not accepted
**Solution:**
- Ensure current password is correct
- Verify new password meets requirements
- Try a different password pattern

**Problem:** Theme doesn't change interface
**Solution:**
- Refresh the browser page
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Log out and back in
