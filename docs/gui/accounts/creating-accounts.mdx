# Creating Accounts

To create a user account, navigate to the account creation page where you will find the "Create your account" form.


The account creation form includes these required fields:

- **Business Email**: Enter a valid business email address that will become your contact email
- **Set Password**: Create a secure password  
  _Minimum 12 characters, containing uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a symbol_
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm
- **Full name**: Enter your complete name
- **Company**: Provide your company or organization name

After filling in all required fields, click the **Create Account** button to finish creating an account. You will be taken to the <Topic id="gui-welcome">**Welcome View**</Topic>.

## Logging Into Your Account

Once you have created an account, you can use the login page to access your account. Enter your email address and password to sign in. If you enter incorrect login credentials, the system will display an error message.

## Password Reset

If you forget your password, you can reset it using the password reset functionality accessible from the login page.


To reset your password:

1. Click the **Forgot Password?** link on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address in the reset form
3. Click **Send Reset Link** to receive the reset email
4. Check your email and click the reset link
5. Enter your new password and confirm it
6. Submit the form to activate your new password
