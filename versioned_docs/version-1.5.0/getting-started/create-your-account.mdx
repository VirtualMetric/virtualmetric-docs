# Create Your Account

Your journey with DataStream begins with creating an account. You have two ways to get started:

## Direct Sign-Up

1. **Navigate to the Sign-Up Page**
   - Go to [DataStream Sign-Up](https://app.eu-west.cloud.virtualmetric.com/sign-up)

2. **Fill in Your Information**
   - **Business email** - This becomes your username for signing in
   - **Set Password** - Create a secure password
   - **Confirm password** - Re-enter your password
   - **Full name** - Enter your first and last name
   - **Company** - Enter your organization name

3. **Create Your Account**
   - Click **Create Account**
   - You'll be taken directly to your DataStream Home dashboard

## Azure Marketplace Sign-Up

If your organization uses Azure, you can sign up through Azure Marketplace:

1. **Find DataStream in Azure Marketplace**
   - Go to [Azure Marketplace](https://azuremarketplace.microsoft.com)
   - Search for "VirtualMetric"
   - Click on **VirtualMetric DataStream**

2. **Subscribe through Azure**
   - Click **Get It Now**
   - Sign in with your Azure account
   - Select your **Subscription** and **Plan**
   - Click **Subscribe**

3. **Complete Setup**
   - Fill in your details on the subscription page
   - Click **Subscribe** to finalize
   - Your DataStream account will be created automatically

## First Sign-In

Once your account is created:
- Use the [Sign-In page](https://app.eu-west.cloud.virtualmetric.com/login)
- Enter your email and password
- Click **Sign in** to access your Home dashboard
